Gesture Guide

Supported Gestures

These are the gestures currently recognized by SignBridga AI. Hold each gesture steadily in front of the camera for best results.

👋

Hello

Open hand with all five fingers extended and spread apart.

Open Palm
👍

Yes

Thumbs up — only the thumb is extended upward, all other fingers curled.

Thumb Up
👎

No

Thumbs down — only the thumb is extended downward, all other fingers curled.

Thumb Down
🆘

Help

Closed fist with only the thumb raised on a flat palm — the ASL sign for "help."

Fist + Thumb
🙏

Thank You

Flat hand with fingers together, slight forward tilt from the chin area.

Flat Hand Forward
🤟

I Love You

Thumb, index finger, and pinky extended. Middle and ring fingers curled in.

ILY Sign
✋

Stop

Palm facing forward with all fingers together and extended — a "halt" signal.

Palm Forward
✌️

Peace

Index and middle fingers extended in a V shape, all other fingers curled.

V Sign
About the Project

Why SignBridga AI Matters

🔴

The Problem

Over 70 million deaf people worldwide use sign language as their primary communication method. Yet most hearing people don't understand sign language, creating a massive communication gap in daily life — at hospitals, schools, workplaces, and public services.

🟢

Our Solution

SignBridga AI bridges this gap by providing instant, free, browser-based sign language translation. No app installation, no paid subscriptions, no special hardware — just open the website, turn on your camera, and start communicating.

🏥

Community Impact

Hospitals can communicate with deaf patients. Schools can include deaf students more effectively. Customer service centers can serve all customers equally. SignBridga AI makes accessibility free and universal.

💡

What's New

Unlike expensive commercial solutions, SignBridga AI runs entirely in the browser using MediaPipe AI — no servers processing your video, no data leaving your device, and no cost to the user. Privacy and accessibility by design.
